SWISS MASTERY

The Art of Precision

Each Chronos Luxe timepiece is the culmination of centuries-old watchmaking traditions combined with cutting-edge technology. Our master craftsmen in Switzerland dedicate over 200 hours to perfecting every detail.

Material Selection

Only the finest 18k gold, platinum, and aerospace-grade titanium are selected for our cases, while sapphire crystals ensure scratch resistance.

Movement Assembly

Each mechanical movement is assembled by hand, with components finished to exacting standards, including perlage, Geneva stripes, and anglage.

Quality Control

Every watch undergoes rigorous testing for precision, water resistance, and power reserve before leaving our atelier.

SINCE 1923

A Legacy of Excellence

For nearly a century, Chronos Luxe has been synonymous with Swiss precision and timeless design.

1923

Founding

Founded in Geneva by master watchmaker Henri Dubois, Chronos Luxe began as a small atelier crafting bespoke timepieces for European nobility.

1948

Innovation

Introduced our first self-winding movement, setting new standards for precision and reliability in automatic watches.

2023

Centenary

Celebrating 100 years of watchmaking excellence with limited edition Masterpiece Collection.

The Masterpiece Collection

To commemorate our centenary, we present 100 exceptional timepieces, each featuring a unique guilloché dial and our most sophisticated movement to date.
